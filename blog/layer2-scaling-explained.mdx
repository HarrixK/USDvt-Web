## Fraud vs Validity Proofs

* **Optimistic rollups** assume blocks are valid; submitters risk stake if fraud proofs succeed.  
* **ZK rollups** provide validity upfront via succinct proofs—higher setup cost, faster withdrawals.

## Bridges ≠ Teleportation

1. Every bridge introduces a new trust model—read audits.  
2. Canonical bridges often subsidise fees; third‑party bridges may not.  
3. UX friction (multiple wallets, message signing) kills adoption faster than fees.
